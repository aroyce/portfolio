Photoshop Mockups

These mockups were created in Photoshop while enrolled in Interface Design I & II. Website URL's for the index pages were assigned by the instructor.

  • Panache BridalIn this long-and-flat index page redesign for www.panachebridals.com, I had to meet the challenge of using the color green and incorporating the theme of outer space.
  • Panache Bridal Mood BoardAliens and space ships didn't seem suitable for a high-end Southern California bridal store. So as I created this mood board, I took inspiration from the ancient mariners who gazed up at the stars.
  • Snow Farm The redesign of the index page for www.snowfarm.org, a New England art camp and retreat center, required maintaining the organization's logo and creating a more personable tone.
  • Affordable Tours This assignment focused on discerning an industry standard for the design layout. After exploring dozens of tourism webpages, I had to emulate one of them. The photos for this redesign of www.affordabletours.com are from my own travels.
  • Market Basket A three-bucket index page redesign plus two advertising layouts.
  • Moss Love Terrarium I established the primary persona for this dominant photo index page redesign as a young woman seeking gifts for her bridal party.
