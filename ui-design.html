User Interface (UI) Design
Washtenaw Community College (WCC)

I created several user interface mock-ups during two interface design classes at WCC. The instructor would typically provide the company name and require a specific page layout style. Progress was evaluated with a design critique in front of the class each week.

Panache Bridal Mood Board and Index Page

I was paired up with another student and we were given the challenge of using the color green and the theme of outer space to incorporate into our design. He and I had to take a different approach to the color and theme. Since aliens and spaceships didn't seem suitable for a high-end Southern California bridal store, I took inspiration from the ancient mariners who gazed up at the stars.

Moss Love Terrarium

A dominant photo index page redesign for a company whose name I pulled from the instructorâ€™s envelope of possibilities. The target user was a young bride seeking wedding decor and gifts for her bridal party.

Market Basket Grocery Store

This assignment was a three-bucket index page redesign; the instructor provided the text and I found the imagery. After three weekly design critiques, I honed in on a pattern that evokes the weave of basketry.

Affordable Tours

Another student and I tackled this long-and-flat index page redesign. We were required to research current travel websites to find common styles for the industry, then each pick a different approach for the Affordable Tours website. I created my mockup with photos from my travels.
