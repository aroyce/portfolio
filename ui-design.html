User Interface (UI) Design
Washtenaw Community College (WCC)

I created several user interface mock-ups in Interface Design I & II classes at WCC. I will have a page that displays these projects as either tiles or a carousel. Each project will consist of a JPG image of the the mockup and the context for the assignment.

Panache Bridal Mood Board and Index Page

I was paired up with another student and we were given the challenge of using the color green and the theme of outer space to incorporate into our design. He and I had to take a different approach to the color and theme. Since aliens and spaceships didn't seem suitable for a high-end Southern California bridal store, I took inspiration from the ancient mariners who gazed up at the stars.

Market Basket Grocery Store

This assignment was a three-bucket index page redesign plus two advertising layouts; the instructor provided the text and I found the imagery. After three, weekly design critiques I was able to hone in on the patterning that evokes the weave of basketry.

Affordable Tours

Another student and I were paired up to tackle this long-and-flat index page redesign. We were required to research current travel websites to find common styles for the industry, then each pick a different approach for the Affordable Tours website. I was able to create my mockup using photos from my travels.
